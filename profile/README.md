## 🚀 What We Do

### **Volis Bridge**
A unified data platform that connects open-source tools with enterprise systems.

- Business analytics & visualization  
- Tailored AI applications and automation  
- Cloud-agnostic and vendor-neutral

### **Volis Build**
A product designed for large-scale construction and infrastructure projects.  
- Digital data capture from the field (offline-first)  
- Automated pipelines and dashboards  
- Standardized reporting across projects  
- Real-time visibility for decision-makers  

---

### **Volis Mining**
An edge-first platform for fleet and operations management in open-pit mining.  
- Data capture at the machine level (production, fuel, maintenance)  
- Operator-friendly, offline-first UX  
- Safety enforcement with Go/No-Go checks  
- Centralized KPIs for operations and executives
